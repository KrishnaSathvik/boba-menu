What is Boba Tea? 🧋

Boba tea (also called bubble tea) is a fun, flavorful drink that originated in Taiwan in the 1980s 🇹🇼. It combines freshly brewed tea with milk, fruit flavors, or both — served ice-cold with chewy tapioca pearls at the bottom!

The "boba" refers to the soft, chewy tapioca balls 🟤 made from cassava root. They sink to the bottom of your drink and you sip them up through a wide straw — it's a drink and a snack in one! 🤤
